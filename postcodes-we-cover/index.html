Postcodes we cover

Every postcode we buy in across South Yorkshire and the surrounding region. If your postcode appears below, we will almost certainly make you a written cash offer within 24 hours. If you are not sure whether your area is covered, get in touch — many edge-case postcodes are still well within our service area.

Quick answer: South Yorkshire Property Buyers covers every S-postcode (S1–S36) across Sheffield, Rotherham, and Barnsley, every DN-postcode across Doncaster (DN1–DN12), plus the immediately surrounding postcodes in north Derbyshire (S40–S45 Chesterfield), north Nottinghamshire (S80–S81 Worksop, DN22 Retford, NG18–NG21 Mansfield), and west Lincolnshire (DN21 Gainsborough). If your address is within 30 miles of Sheffield city centre, we almost certainly buy there.

Sheffield postcodes (S1–S36)

We cover every Sheffield S-postcode in the city, suburbs, and surrounding villages. The largest concentration of activity is in S6 (Hillsborough corridor), S7 (Nether Edge), S10 (Crookes, Broomhill), S11 (Ecclesall, Sharrow), and S5 (Shiregreen, Firth Park), but we are equally active in S20 Mosborough, the south-east stretch towards Beighton, and S35 (Stocksbridge, Chapeltown) on the city's northern edge.

Rotherham postcodes (S60–S66)

Rotherham's postcode area runs from the town centre out to the eastern villages and the southern stretch towards Maltby. Barnsley postcodes (S70–S75)

Barnsley's S70 to S75 postcode zone covers the town centre and the surrounding former mining communities and rural fringe. The S72 area around Cudworth, Royston, and Grimethorpe sits at the heart of the old coalfield. S75 reaches west towards Penistone and Silkstone, where the housing stock is older and more rural in character.

Common questions

What postcodes do you cover?

We cover all S-postcodes (S1 to S36) across Sheffield, Rotherham, and Barnsley, every DN-postcode in Doncaster (DN1 to DN12), and the surrounding area including S40–S45 Chesterfield, S80–S81 Worksop, DN22 Retford, NG18–NG21 Mansfield, and DN21 Gainsborough.

Do you only buy in central postcodes or also in smaller villages?

We buy across every postcode in our coverage zone — town centres, suburbs, ex-mining villages, rural fringe, and everywhere in between. Mexborough, Goldthorpe, Penistone, Tickhill, Stocksbridge, and Maltby are all within our standard service area.

What if my postcode is on the edge of your coverage area?

If you are within 30 miles of Sheffield city centre we will almost always buy. Contact us and we will confirm before you commit time to the process.

Do you cover the whole of South Yorkshire?

Yes. We cover all four metropolitan boroughs (Sheffield, Rotherham, Doncaster, Barnsley) plus the surrounding postcode areas in north Derbyshire, north Nottinghamshire, and west Lincolnshire.
