## 3. The Offer

**Typical cash offer:** 80–85% of market value. This reflects the trade-off the seller is making — speed and certainty of completion in exchange for a below-market price. Reference: https://propertybuyerstoday.co.uk/sell-house-fast/

**Completion speed:**
- Fastest completed: 7 days
- Standard fast completion: as little as 7 days
- Flexible completions also available — can align with a specific date (e.g. probate, moving, divorce settlement)

**Legal fees:** Covered by SYPB when the seller uses recommended solicitors. Sellers may also use their own solicitor if preferred — this is their right and should never be discouraged.

**Property condition:** Any condition accepted. Preference is for properties requiring renovation.

**Property types:**
- Primarily residential
- Commercial considered (experience exists, but residential is the core expertise)
- All residential types: houses, flats

**Offer validity:** 14 days from the date made. The offer is guaranteed subject to the information provided at the time. Any material changes discovered during conveyancing (e.g. title issues, structural discoveries) may result in a price adjustment.

## 8. Tone of Voice

**Core tone:** Understanding, reliable, straight-talking, local.

We take the time to understand what a seller is going through. We don't rush them with sales pressure. We focus on what they need — and deliver it.

**The three brand pillars:**
1. **Speed** — we move fast and don't waste time
2. **Certainty** — we say what we'll do and we do it
3. **Reliability** — sellers can trust us to follow through

**Write like this:**
- Warm but direct — we understand your situation, here's what we can do
- Plain English — no jargon, no legal language in marketing copy
- Local — reference South Yorkshire, the areas, the communities
- Confident — we've done this before, we know how it works

**Do not write like this:**
- Corporate and cold
- Overpromising or vague ("we'll get you the best price" — we won't, and we're honest about that)
- Slow or uncertain — anything that implies hesitation, delays, or complications
- National in tone — SYPB is a local business, not a call centre operation

---

## 9. Brand Guardrails

**Always:**
- Lead with speed, certainty, and local knowledge
- Be honest about the 80–85% offer — frame it as a fair trade for speed and certainty, not a lowball
- Reference the 7-day completion as the fastest option, not the guaranteed standard
- Keep legal fee coverage conditional on using recommended solicitors

**Never:**
- Claim we buy at full market value
- Promise a specific price before assessment
- Imply there are no circumstances under which an offer could change
- Use language that suggests pressure, urgency manipulation, or exploitation of vulnerable sellers
- Reference reviews or testimonials that don't exist yet
