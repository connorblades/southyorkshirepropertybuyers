Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect your personal information.

Last updated: 9 April 2026

Who we are

South Yorkshire Property Buyers is a trading name of Bullseye Properties Ltd, registered in England and Wales (Company No. 14869608). This website is operated as a lead generation platform for Bullseye Properties Ltd, which purchases residential property directly for cash.

When you submit an enquiry through this website, your information is received and processed by Bullseye Properties Ltd. References to "we", "us" or "our" in this policy refer to Bullseye Properties Ltd.

For any data-related queries, please use our contact form or call us on 07793 063734.

What personal data we collect

When you submit an enquiry form on this website, we collect:

  • Your full name
  • Your phone number
  • Your email address (if provided)
  • The address or postcode of the property you wish to sell
  • Details about your situation and timeline (if provided)

We do not collect any special category data (such as health information) and we do not collect payment details through this website.

How we use your information

We use the information you submit to:

  • Contact you regarding your property and provide a cash purchase offer
  • Respond to your enquiry by phone, email or SMS
  • Progress a property purchase if you wish to proceed

Our lawful basis for processing your data is legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f) UK GDPR) — specifically, to respond to an enquiry you have actively submitted requesting a cash offer on your property.

Who we share your data with

Your enquiry data is passed to the following:

  • Bullseye Properties Ltd — the data controller and purchasing entity, who will contact you directly regarding your property
  • GoHighLevel (Lead Connector) — a CRM platform used to manage and follow up on enquiries. GoHighLevel processes data in accordance with their own privacy policy and is subject to standard contractual clauses for data transfers

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not share your data with estate agents, mortgage brokers or any other commercial parties without your consent.

How long we keep your data

If a property purchase completes, we retain relevant records for up to 7 years in accordance with standard UK financial and property record-keeping requirements.

If your enquiry does not result in a purchase, we will retain your contact details for up to 12 months in case you wish to revisit a sale. You can request deletion at any time (see below).

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct any inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (the "right to be forgotten")
  • Restrict how we process your data
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Data portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us via our contact form or call 07793 063734. We will respond within 30 days.

If you are not satisfied with how we handle your data, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk or by calling 0303 123 1113.

Cookies and local storage

This website uses browser local storage to remember your preferred colour scheme (light or dark mode). This stores a single value on your device and is not used for tracking or analytics purposes. No third-party cookies are set by this website.

Links to other websites

This website contains links to external sites (including our social media profiles). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and recommend you review their policies independently.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page will reflect when it was last revised. Continued use of this website following any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
