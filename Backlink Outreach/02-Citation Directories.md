# Citation Directory Submission Checklist

NAP-consistent listings across UK business directories. Submit using identical details every time:

```
Business name: South Yorkshire Property Buyers
Phone: 07739 063734
Website: https://southyorkshirepropertybuyers.com
Email: connor@bullseyeproperties.co.uk
Trading name of: Bullseye Properties Ltd (Companies House 14869608)
Area served: Sheffield, Rotherham, Doncaster, Barnsley, Chesterfield, Worksop, Retford, Gainsborough
Category: Cash House Buyers / Property Buyers / Quick Sale Property
```

**Address note:** SYPB has no public street address. For directories that require one, use the Bullseye Properties Ltd registered company address from Companies House (verify on register before submitting).

## Submission technique

- Use the same browser session and form-autofill where possible
- Open all 13 Tier 1 sites in tabs, blast through in 60–90 minutes
- Skip any that ask for unverifiable details (rate card, employee count) — move on
- After each submission, paste the listing URL back into the Status column above
- If a directory creates a profile page with your details, you've earned a citation even if no link

## Why this matters less than press

Directory citations are a baseline trust signal. They confirm to Google that "South Yorkshire Property Buyers" is a real business operating where it claims. They rarely move rankings on their own. The press and journalist work in `03-Outreach Emails.md` and `05-Press Release...md` is where the actual ranking lift comes from.
