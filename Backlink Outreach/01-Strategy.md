# SYPB Backlink Strategy — May 2026

Goal: build a clean, credible link profile that lifts pages from position ~55 toward page 1.

## Priority order (highest ROI first)

### Tier 1 — Local press (Sheffield Star, Doncaster Free Press, Rotherham Advertiser)
The single highest-leverage link source for SYPB. A quote in The Star or Doncaster Free Press is:
- A dofollow link from a high-authority domain (DR 70+)
- Topically perfect (local business, property, finance)
- Reusable — once a journalist trusts you, they come back

**Approach:** offer yourself as a quotable local source on Renters' Rights Act effects, mortgage arrears trends, the buy-to-let exit, repossession avoidance. UK Finance Q1 2026 mortgage arrears data drops 14 May 2026 — that's the immediate hook.

### Tier 2 — Free journalist request platforms
Sign up once, scan daily, respond to 1–3 queries per week. Each response that lands is a featured-quote backlink in mainstream UK media.

- **Source of Sources (sosrequests.com)** — free, built by HARO's original founder, low spam
- **Qwoted (qwoted.com)** — free tier, vets sources, lots of UK property queries
- **Featured (featured.com)** — free, curated expert roundups for Fortune/Yahoo/Fast Company
- **#journorequest on X** — free, just monitor the hashtag
- **Help a B2B Writer (helpab2bwriter.com)** — free, smaller volume

Skip paid ones (ResponseSource £625/category, Connectively) until later.

### Tier 3 — Property industry guest posts
Pitch contributed articles. Each placement = 1 dofollow link from a DR 40+ relevant site.

- **Property118 (property118.com)** — landlord community, accepts contributed pieces
- **Landlord Today (landlordtoday.co.uk)** — accepts opinion pieces from industry experts
- **MoneyMagpie (moneymagpie.com)** — personal finance, takes property contributions
- **The Advisory (theadvisory.co.uk)** — most authoritative quick-sale-specific UK site, harder to crack but valuable

### Tier 4 — Local Chamber memberships (paid)
Cheapest reliable dofollow link from a credible local-business domain.

- **Doncaster Chamber** — Bronze £347.04+VAT/yr, Silver £609.96+VAT/yr (best value tier with member profile page)
- **Sheffield Chamber (SCCI)** — pricing on request
- **Barnsley & Rotherham Chamber** — pricing on request

Treat as a long-term investment: link + networking + occasional referral leads.

### Tier 5 — Citation directories
NAP-consistent baseline. Low SEO impact individually, useful collectively for trust signals. See [02-Citation Directories.md](02-Citation%20Directories.md).

### Tier 6 — Strategic partnerships
Slowest, but highest LTV (links + leads):
- Local probate solicitors (could refer cases SYPB would also handle — link exchange in resource pages)
- Local IFAs and debt advisors
- Local letting agents seeing tired landlords

Build over months, not weeks.

## Realistic 90-day target

If executed consistently:
- Week 1–2: 20+ citations live, 1 press quote pitched
- Month 1: 1 press placement, 5 directory listings live, signed up to 4 journalist platforms
- Month 2: 2–3 press placements, 1 guest post placed, chamber membership live
- Month 3: 5+ referring domains from real editorial sources, first ranking lifts visible in GSC

A fresh local business going from 0 to 10 strong referring domains in 90 days will see search position lifts. That's the realistic target.
