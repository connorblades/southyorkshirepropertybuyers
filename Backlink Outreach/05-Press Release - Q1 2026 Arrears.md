## Why this beats a cold pitch

- Tied to a public news event (the data drop) → editor knows there's reader interest
- Local-angle on a national story → exactly what regional papers want
- Real data + named source → not press-release fluff
- Specific case studies offered → ready-made human interest paragraph
- Multiple recipients pitched same morning → at least one likely to bite
