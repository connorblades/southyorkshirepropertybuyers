Why sell your Sheffield property to us?

Selling a house in Sheffield through an estate agent takes an average of four to six months from listing to completion. That assumes no chain collapses, no survey surprises, and no buyer pulling out. For many Sheffield homeowners, that wait simply is not an option.

What we pay for Sheffield properties

We offer between 80 and 90% of current market value in exchange for speed, certainty, and zero fees. That trade-off is more valuable than it might initially appear.

A Sheffield property sitting on the market for six months while you pay mortgage, council tax, and utilities can easily cost you £6,000 to £10,000 in running costs, before you subtract estate agent commission. In many cases, the net amount you receive from us is comparable to or better than what you would receive after a traditional sale once all costs are accounted for.

We will always be transparent about what we can offer and why, so you can make an informed decision.

Frequently asked questions: Sheffield

How quickly can you buy my Sheffield property?

Our fastest Sheffield completion has been 7 days. A more typical timeline is 14 to 21 days. We can also work to a longer timescale if you need more time to arrange your move.

Do I need to clean or repair the property first?

No. We buy Sheffield properties in any condition, including properties that have been empty for years, properties with structural issues, and properties that have been let and not maintained. You do not need to do anything to the property before we buy it.

Will you really stick to your offer?

Yes. We do not use a high initial offer to get you engaged and then reduce it later. The written offer we make is the price we complete at.

What Sheffield postcodes do you cover?

We buy across all S-postcodes: S1 through to S36, and beyond into the surrounding areas. If your property is in Sheffield or on its outskirts, we will buy it.

Are there any fees?

None. We cover all legal costs through our panel solicitors. You pay nothing throughout the process.
