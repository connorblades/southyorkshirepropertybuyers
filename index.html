Divorce & Separation

A shared property ties you to the past. We cut through the emotional and legal complexity by purchasing quickly, so both parties can walk away cleanly and start fresh. We handle everything with sensitivity and discretion.

Dealing with a loved one's estate is already overwhelming. We work directly with solicitors and executors to buy probate properties cleanly and quickly, with no long chains and no waiting around for buyers who may pull out.

Common Questions

Frequently asked questions.

Everything you need to know about selling your house fast for cash in South Yorkshire.

How much below market value will you offer?

We typically offer between 80–90% of market value in exchange for speed, certainty, and zero fees. On a £130,000 property, once you factor in 9 months of mortgage payments, council tax, utilities and estate agent fees, many sellers walk away with more by selling to us. See our comparison table above for the real numbers.

How quickly can you complete?

Our fastest completion was 7 days. Most sales complete within 2–4 weeks depending on solicitor availability and your circumstances. If you have a repossession date or any other deadline, tell us upfront and we will do everything we can to work around it.

Do I need to make repairs or clean the property first?

Absolutely not. We buy in any condition: full of contents, in need of full renovation, or with serious structural issues. You do not need to lift a finger. We have dealt with fire-damaged homes, properties with severe subsidence, Japanese knotweed, and everything in between.

Are there any fees or hidden costs?

None whatsoever. No estate agent fees, no survey costs, no conveyancing fees. We cover all legal costs. The price we agree is the price you receive. No last-minute reductions, no surprises at completion.

Do you cover all of South Yorkshire?

Yes. We cover Sheffield, Rotherham, Doncaster, Barnsley, and all surrounding towns and villages. This includes Maltby, Wath-upon-Dearne, Mexborough, Thorne, Conisbrough, Penistone, Wombwell, Goldthorpe and everywhere in between. If you.re unsure, just ask. We cover more than most.

Can you buy my property if it has tenants in it?

Yes. We regularly buy tenanted properties, including those with non-paying tenants or ongoing Section 21 proceedings. We take on the tenancy situation as part of the purchase. You do not need to resolve it first or wait for vacant possession.

Is my enquiry completely confidential?

Completely. We understand that many clients are dealing with sensitive situations, including financial difficulty, relationship breakdown, probate. Everything you tell us stays strictly between us and our solicitors. We will never contact neighbours, put up a For Sale board, or disclose your circumstances to anyone.

What if I am already in repossession proceedings?

We can still help, even if a repossession date has already been set. In many cases we can complete quickly enough to settle your mortgage arrears before the lender takes possession. Contact us immediately with your repossession date and we will tell you honestly whether we can help in time.
