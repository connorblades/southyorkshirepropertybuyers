We Understand Your Situation

Life doesn't always go to plan.

Whatever brought you here, we've helped people in exactly your position. No judgement. Just a fast, straightforward solution.

Facing Repossession

Mortgage arrears mounting and the lender is threatening to repossess? We can complete before your repossession date, clear your debt, and protect your credit history from further damage. Time is critical and we know how to move fast.

Divorce & Separation

A shared property ties you to the past. We cut through the emotional and legal complexity by purchasing quickly, so both parties can walk away cleanly and start fresh. We handle everything with sensitivity and discretion.

Probate & Inherited Estates

Dealing with a loved one's estate is already overwhelming. We work directly with solicitors and executors to buy probate properties cleanly and quickly, with no long chains and no waiting around for buyers who may pull out.

Title & Legal Issues

Restrictive covenants, missing deeds, boundary disputes, flying freeholds. These are problems that scare off traditional buyers. We have the experience to work through complications others will not touch.

Financial Pressure

Whether it's debt, job loss, or a change in circumstances, a sale that drags on only makes things worse. We work to your timeline, often completing in under two weeks, so you can regain control of your finances.

Problem Properties

Unmortgageable, structural issues, subsidence, Japanese knotweed. We buy properties the open market will not touch. No surveys, no pulled offers, no last-minute surprises. We make one offer and we stick to it.

About Us

South Yorkshire's local property buyers.

We are a small, experienced team of property investors based right here in South Yorkshire. We are not a national call centre. When you contact us, you speak directly with the decision-maker, with no middlemen and no waiting on approvals from head office.

We have been buying residential and commercial property across Sheffield, Rotherham, Doncaster, Barnsley, and the surrounding areas for years. We know this market, we know the local solicitors, and we know how to move quickly when it matters.

We pay a fair cash price, typically 80 to 90 % of market value, in exchange for speed, certainty, and zero hassle. For many people in difficult circumstances, that trade-off is absolutely worth it, and we will always be honest with you about what to expect.

We can also cover your legal costs. We work with our own panel of solicitors who know exactly how we operate, which keeps things moving quickly. If you would prefer to use your own solicitor, that is absolutely fine too. We can make offers that include a contribution towards those costs, so there is nothing standing in your way.
