What matters to us

We are clear about the offer from the very first conversation. The figure we put in writing is the figure we intend to complete at. We do not use an inflated initial offer to build trust and then negotiate down before exchange. Sellers deserve to know what they are accepting.

Most people who contact us are dealing with something difficult: a property they cannot sell, a financial situation with real pressure, or a personal circumstance that makes the usual selling process impractical. We try to be straightforward with people in those situations, not persuasive.

We do not follow up repeatedly if someone decides not to proceed. If our offer is not right for you, or if you want more time to consider, that is your decision to make. We will be here if you want to come back to it.

We are a local South Yorkshire business. Our reputation matters to us in a way that a national operation's might not. We want every seller who deals with us to feel that the process was fair, clear, and handled with respect.

What our sellers tell us they valued most

When we speak with sellers at the end of a completed sale, we ask what stood out about the process. A few things come up consistently.

  • Clarity from day one. Sellers tell us they appreciated knowing exactly what the offer was, how it was calculated, and what the process involved, without having to chase for information or read between the lines.
  • Not being pressured. People in difficult situations are often already under stress. Not feeling pushed to decide quickly made a significant difference to how they experienced the process.
  • Speed compared to previous attempts. Several sellers had been through months of open-market attempts, viewings, and fall-throughs before contacting us. The contrast in pace was often striking.
  • One point of contact. Dealing with one consistent person throughout, rather than being passed between departments or call handlers, made the process feel manageable.
  • No viewings, no estate agents, no fall-throughs. For sellers who had experienced a chain collapse or a prolonged market listing, the certainty of a cash sale was itself the main benefit, separate from any price consideration.

Common questions about our reviews

Are your reviews genuine?

Yes. We do not publish or request fake reviews. Every review on our Google Business profile comes from someone who has sold a property to us.

How can I leave a review?

Search for "South Yorkshire Property Buyers" on Google and click the "Write a review" option on our Business profile. We genuinely value honest feedback.

What if I had a negative experience?

We want to know. Contact us directly at hello@southyorkshirepropertybuyers.com and we will work to resolve any concern.

Can you give me references from previous sellers?

We prioritise the privacy of our sellers. Most people who sell in difficult circumstances prefer not to be named as a reference. Our Google reviews represent sellers who have chosen to make their feedback public.
