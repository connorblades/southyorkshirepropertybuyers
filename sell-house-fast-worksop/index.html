Types of Worksop property we buy

Worksop has a distinct split between older mining community housing and newer private development. Rhodesia and Langold have large numbers of former colliery terraces. Carlton-in-Lindrick and Shireoaks have more private housing from the 1970s onwards. The villages to the south, including Welbeck, Cuckney and Budby, have rural and agricultural properties. We buy all of it, regardless of condition or history.

  • Ex-council properties in Worksop town

  • Properties with ground stability or mining legacy concerns
  • Flats, apartments and leasehold properties
  • Properties with structural issues or in need of full renovation
  • Tenanted properties including those with problem tenancies

What we pay for Worksop properties

We offer between 80 and 90% of current market value in exchange for speed, certainty, and zero fees. That trade-off is more valuable than it might initially appear.

Worksop properties can take longer to sell than the regional average, particularly in the former mining areas where buyer demand from owner-occupiers is thinner. A property sitting empty for several months accrues mortgage payments, council tax and utility standing charges. Add estate agent commission and the gap between our offer and what you would net from a traditional sale is often smaller than sellers expect.

We will always be transparent about what we can offer and why, so you can make an informed decision.
