## Target Audience Summary

Motivated sellers — people who need or want to sell quickly due to difficult personal or financial circumstances. They are not browsing casually. They are stressed, often embarrassed, and looking for a solution that removes the burden.

**Age range:** 35–65 primary, 25–75 secondary
**Location targeting:** South Yorkshire — Sheffield, Rotherham, Doncaster, Barnsley + 15-mile radius
**Interests to target:** Mortgage advice, debt management, property selling, Rightmove, Zoopla, estate agents, landlord forums, Citizens Advice, repossession, probate, divorce
**Life events to target:** Recently divorced, recently bereaved, financial difficulty signals
**Custom audiences:** Retarget website visitors (pixel), lookalike from enquiry form completions

### Hook Option A — Urgency (Repossession / Debt)
> **"If your mortgage lender has sent you a letter — watch this."**

### Hook Option B — Empathy (General motivated seller)

## Visual Style Direction

Align with the website's visual identity:

- **Colour palette:** Primary green (`#1c6b40`) for overlays, badges, buttons. Dark (`#111a14`) for backgrounds. White text on dark/green backgrounds.
- **Font:** Inter — bold for headlines, regular for body
- **Tone:** Clean, professional, not flashy. No stock-photo smiles. Real properties, real South Yorkshire locations if possible.
- **Logo placement:** Bottom right or top left. Use `logo-light.png` (white version) on dark backgrounds.
- **CTA button style:** Green background (`#1c6b40`), white text, rounded corners — matching the website button style
- **No:** Overly aggressive red text, countdown timers, fake urgency graphics, "CALL NOW" style creative

## What NOT to Include

- No fake reviews or fabricated testimonials
- No countdown timers unless real
- No promises of specific offer percentages in the ad (keep on website)
- No aggressive "you're losing money every day" framing
- No national comparisons ("better than Purple Bricks" etc.)
