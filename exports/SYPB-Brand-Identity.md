## 5. Tone of Voice

The brand tone is **direct, honest, and empathetic**. We are speaking to people in genuinely difficult situations — repossession, divorce, bereavement, financial hardship — and the copy must reflect that.

### Core Principles

- **Plain English always** — no jargon, no estate agent speak
- **Honest about the trade-off** — we pay below market value and we say so clearly
- **Empathetic not patronising** — acknowledge the difficulty without being condescending
- **Specific not vague** — use real numbers, real timescales, real comparisons
- **Local not corporate** — we are a small South Yorkshire team, not a national call centre
- **Decisive not pushy** — confident, clear CTAs but never pressure-based language

### Phrases We Use

- *"No estate agent fees. No surveys. No delays."*
- *"Free, no obligation. We respond within 24 hours."*
- *"We buy in any condition, any situation."*
- *"The price we agree is the price you receive."*
- *"No last-minute reductions, no surprises at completion."*
- *"You speak directly with the decision-maker."*
- *"We cover all legal costs."*
- *"As little as 7 days."*
- *"Whatever brought you here, we have helped people in exactly your position."*

### Phrases to Avoid

- "Market-leading" or superlatives without evidence
- "Guaranteed" (avoid if legally ambiguous)
- "We understand how you feel" (too hollow — show it through specifics instead)
- Exclamation marks (undermine the trust-focused tone)
- "Click here" (use descriptive CTAs instead)

## 13. Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How much below market value will you offer?**
A: We typically offer between 80–85% of market value in exchange for speed, certainty, and zero fees. On a £130,000 property, once you factor in 9 months of mortgage payments, council tax, utilities and estate agent fees, many sellers walk away with more by selling to us.

**Q: How quickly can you complete?**
A: Our fastest completion was 7 days. Most sales complete within 2–4 weeks depending on solicitor availability and your circumstances. If you have a repossession date or any other deadline, tell us upfront and we will do everything we can to work around it.

**Q: Do I need to make repairs or clean the property first?**
A: Absolutely not. We buy in any condition: full of contents, in need of full renovation, or with serious structural issues. You do not need to lift a finger. We have dealt with fire-damaged homes, properties with severe subsidence, Japanese knotweed, and everything in between.

**Q: Are there any fees or hidden costs?**
A: None whatsoever. No estate agent fees, no survey costs, no conveyancing fees. We cover all legal costs. The price we agree is the price you receive. No last-minute reductions, no surprises at completion.

**Q: Do you cover all of South Yorkshire?**
A: Yes. We cover Sheffield, Rotherham, Doncaster, Barnsley, and all surrounding towns and villages. This includes Maltby, Wath-upon-Dearne, Mexborough, Thorne, Conisbrough, Penistone, Wombwell, Goldthorpe and everywhere in between.

**Q: Is the offer really free and non-binding?**
A: Yes. There is absolutely no cost to receiving an offer from us and no obligation whatsoever to accept it. We will never pressure you. If you decide the offer is not right for you, that is completely fine. There are no strings attached.

**Q: Will you buy my property even if it needs work?**
A: Yes. We buy properties in any condition, including those that are unmortgageable, have structural issues, need full renovation, or have been empty for years. Condition does not prevent a sale with us. It simply informs the offer we make.
