# South Yorkshire Property Buyers — Target Audience Personas
## For Meta Ad Targeting & Video Creative Direction

---

## Overview

These are the real people the ads are speaking to. Each persona represents a distinct motivated seller segment. Use these to inform video tone, hook selection, caption copy, and Meta audience targeting.

---

## Persona 1 — The Repossession Risk

**Name:** Mark, 48, Rotherham
**Situation:** Lost his job 8 months ago. Has fallen 4 months behind on mortgage payments. Received a formal letter from his lender. He's scared, embarrassed, and not sure what his options are. He's been ignoring the problem hoping it resolves itself.

**What he's thinking:**
- "I don't want people to know what's happening"
- "I've left it too late"
- "I don't know if selling would even help at this point"
- "I can't afford a solicitor"

**What he needs to hear:**
- You still have options — selling before repossession can clear the debt
- We move fast and can work around your deadline
- This is confidential — no judgement
- We cover the legal costs so there's nothing to pay upfront

**Meta targeting signals:**
- Searched for: mortgage arrears help, repossession advice, stop repossession, Citizens Advice mortgage
- Interests: Debt advice, StepChange, Citizens Advice, mortgage calculators
- Life events: Recent financial difficulty

**Best hook:** *"If your mortgage lender has sent you a letter — watch this."*
**Best format:** Facebook Feed, 30–45 seconds, empathetic tone

- Sensitive, discreet, no pressure

**Meta targeting signals:**
- Interests: Divorce solicitors, legal advice, family law, property settlement
- Life events: Recently divorced / relationship change
- Searched for: sell house during divorce, jointly owned property divorce

**Best hook:** *"A shared property ties you to the past. We help you move on."*
**Best format:** Instagram Feed or Reels, 15–30 seconds, calm tone

## Universal Motivators (All Personas)

All motivated sellers share these underlying desires regardless of their specific situation:

1. **Speed** — they need this resolved, not dragged out
2. **Certainty** — they've been let down before (or fear being let down)
3. **Simplicity** — they don't want more complexity in an already stressful situation
4. **Discretion** — most are embarrassed about their situation and don't want it public
5. **Local trust** — they don't trust faceless national companies

Address at least 3 of these 5 in every ad.
